* Music Streaming: Do not use any music streaming platform. 
  Get the music you like on your local machine by buying it 
  from the artists/labels directly or via bandcamp etc. and 
  listen to it locally via a music player like [cmus](https://cmus.github.io/). 
  Spotify does not deserve to be supported for various reasons.
  [Here are a few](https://stallman.org/spotify.html).
